Who Can Use This

We're building a space for adults to be real with each other. If you're under 18, we'll have to delete your account if we find out. Nothing personal, your time is better spent outdoors and in nature than online on social media anyway.

By signing up, you're telling us:

How You Get In

Why? Because when everyone's accountable to someone, people behave better. It's not exclusivity for its own sake. It's quality control.

Your account is your responsibility. If someone else uses it and does something stupid, that's on you.

Don't Be Terrible

hum is voice-first. You talk, you don't type. That's the whole point.

Your content is yours. We don't own it. But by posting, you're giving us permission to show it to other users and keep the app running. That's literally the service.

Don't post:

  • Anything illegal, threatening, or harassing
  • Other people's stuff without permission
  • Spam or scams
  • Porn or explicit content
  • Stuff that promotes violence or self-harm
  • Impersonation of others

Use common sense. Would you say it to someone's face at a dinner party? No? Don't say it here.

Voice Recordings

Quick reminder: your voice posts and DMs get transcribed after 24 hours, then the audio is deleted forever. Only the text sticks around (for moderation and search).

This isn't a bug. It's the feature. Be present, not permanent.

Moderation

We moderate content. We have to. But we're not watching everything in real time, so if you see something bad, report it.

If you break the rules:

  • First offense: We might just remove the content and warn you

We make these calls ourselves. It's not a democracy. If you don't like it, there are other apps.

Quests

Quests are your personal goals. Minimum 30 days, daily check-ins.

You can make them public (anyone can join), private (invite only), or solo (just you).

Important: We're a tool, not a miracle. Whether you hit your goals is entirely up to you. We're just here to help you stay accountable.

Camera and Mic

We need access to your camera and microphone. That's how a voice-first app works.

We don't access your photo gallery. Every photo on hum is taken in the moment. No digging through your camera roll for the perfect shot from 2019.
