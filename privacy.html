Privacy Policy

The version you'll actually read.

Last updated: December 21, 2025

We don't sell your data. We don't show you ads. We don't track you across the internet. We collect what we need to make the app work, nothing more. Your voice recordings get deleted after 24 hours. We're not trying to build a profile of you to sell to advertisers because, well, we don't have advertisers.

That's the gist. The rest is the details.

What We Collect

Here's everything we know about you:

When you sign up:

  • Your name and email (so we can, you know, contact you)
  • Your profile picture (taken in-app, not from your gallery)
  • A voice intro if you want to record one
  • Who referred you (for the waitlist leaderboard)

When you use the app:

  • Voice recordings (posts, comments, DMs)
  • Photos you take in-app (we can't access your camera roll)
  • Your quests and progress
  • Who you interact with and how

Automatically:

  • Your device type and browser
  • Your IP address (gives us rough location)
  • How you use the app (what features, how often)

What Happens to Your Voice

This is the important bit.

When you record a voice post or DM, here's the lifecycle:

  • 0-24 hours: Your actual voice is stored and playable
  • After 24 hours: We transcribe it to text, then delete the audio file forever
  • From then on: Only the text transcript exists

Why? Because storing audio is expensive, and frankly, the ephemeral nature makes people more authentic. You're not building a permanent archive of everything you've ever said.

Your voice intro on your profile stays until you delete it. That one's meant to stick around.

Why We Collect It

Pretty simple:

  • To make the app work (obviously)
  • To show you your feed, your quests, your streaks
  • To send you notifications when someone interacts with your stuff
  • To figure out what features people actually use so we can make them better
  • To stop spammers and bad actors

We're not analyzing your voice to sell you things. We're not building psychological profiles. We just want to make a social app that doesn't suck.

Who Sees Your Data

We don't sell your data. Full stop.

The only third parties who touch your data:

  • Microsoft Azure: Hosts our servers (in Australia). Also does our speech-to-text transcription. They don't keep your audio after processing.
  • Stripe: If you become a supporter, they handle the payment. We never see your card number.
  • The law: If someone with a valid legal order demands your data, we have to comply. We'd fight unreasonable requests, but we're not going to jail for you. Sorry.

That's it. No data brokers. No ad networks. No "partners" who want to "enhance your experience."

Where Your Data Lives

Everything is stored on Microsoft Azure servers in Australia. If you're not in Australia, your data crosses borders to get here. That's how the internet works.

How We Protect It

  • Everything's encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • We use proper authentication (JWT tokens, not security through obscurity)
  • We don't store passwords in plain text (obviously)
  • We limit who on our team can access what

No system is unhackable. We do our best, but if you're planning a coup, maybe don't discuss it on hum.

Your Rights

You can:

  • See your data: Ask us what we have on you
  • Fix your data: Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your data: Nuke your account and everything with it
  • Export your data: Take it with you if you leave
  • Turn off notifications: We get it, notifications are annoying

Email hello@hum-social.com for any of this. We'll actually respond.

About That Camera Access

Yes, we need your camera and microphone. It's a voice-first app with photos. Kind of essential.

What we don't do: access your photo gallery. Every photo on hum was taken in the moment, in the app. No curated selfies from three years ago. No stolen memes. Just real, right-now content.

You can revoke camera/mic permissions anytime in your phone settings. The app will be pretty useless, but it's your call.

Kids

hum is for adults (18+). If you're under 18, please don't sign up. If we find out you did, we'll delete your account. Nothing personal, it's just the rules.

If you're a parent and your kid somehow got on here, email us and we'll sort it out.

Changes

We might update this policy. If we make big changes, we'll tell you (email or in-app notification). If you keep using hum after that, you're agreeing to the new terms. Standard stuff.

Questions?

Email us: hello@hum-social.com

We're real humans. We'll write back.
