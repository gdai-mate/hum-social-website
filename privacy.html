Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to hüm's Privacy Policy. This policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our voice-first accountability platform.

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to transparency about our data practices and giving you control over your information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username and display name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Voice introduction (optional)
  • Bio and interests
  • Referral code (for registration tracking)

Content You Create

  • Voice recordings (posts, comments, DMs, voice intros)
  • Quest information (titles, tasks, progress data)
  • High fives (likes with optional voice captions)
  • Photos (profile pictures only - no gallery access)

Usage Data

  • Quest check-in history and streaks
  • Social interactions (vibes, high fives, comments)
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage patterns and feature engagement

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the hüm platform
  • Enable social features (feed, profiles, DMs, notifications)
  • Track quest progress and calculate streaks
  • Send notifications about platform activity
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Provide customer support

4. Voice Data and Audio Processing

24-Hour Audio Lifecycle: Voice recordings in posts and DMs have a 24-hour lifecycle. After 24 hours:

  • Audio is transcribed to text using Azure Cognitive Services
  • Original audio file is permanently deleted
  • Text transcript is retained for content moderation and search

Voice Profile (Optional): Your voice introduction recording is stored indefinitely unless you delete it. It is only played when someone visits your profile.

Third-Party Processing: We use Azure Cognitive Services (Microsoft) for speech-to-text transcription. Microsoft processes audio according to their privacy policy and does not retain audio data after transcription.

5. Data Storage and Security

Storage Location: Your data is stored on Microsoft Azure servers in Australia (East Australia region) to comply with data sovereignty requirements.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted data storage
  • JWT-based authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Role-based access controls

While we take security seriously, no system is completely secure. You use hüm at your own risk.

6. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Public Content: Public posts and quests are visible to other users
  • Service Providers: Azure (hosting), Microsoft (speech-to-text)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and marketing
  • Visibility Control: Set quest visibility (Public/Private/Solo)

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@hum-social.com or use the in-app settings.

8. Camera and Microphone Access

Required Permissions: hüm requires camera and microphone access for:

  • Recording voice posts and comments
  • Sending voice direct messages
  • Capturing profile pictures in-app
  • Recording voice introductions

No Gallery Access: We intentionally do not access your photo gallery. All photos must be captured using the in-app camera to ensure authenticity and prevent misuse.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, but this will limit app functionality.

9. Age Requirements and Minors

hüm is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18.

If we discover that we have collected information from a user under 18, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@hum-social.com.

10. International Users

hüm is operated from Australia and data is stored on Australian servers. By using hüm, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in Australia, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email. Continued use of hüm after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
